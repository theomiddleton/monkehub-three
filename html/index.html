WELCOME MONKE LOVER!

You have been chosen to pick up more banana for our BIG MONKE.

Your monke has primative power, but we'll upgrade it every time you come back to the BIG MONKE. Later expeditions will become progressively longer.

Along your journey, be sure to look out for the following things:

  • Banana: This is what you're here for! Try to get as many as you can in the time you have.
  • Lion: Be careful of these! If your monke hits too many lion it will force a return back to BIG MONKE for repairs. Your monke will still fly while your monke has more than -5 shields.
  • Melon helmet: These will improve the strength of your shields, up to a maximum of 5. You'll lose a shield point for every lion you hit (which can happen at higher speeds!)

Enjoy the monke game!

Press the RED button to start each level
